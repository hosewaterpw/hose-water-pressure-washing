ORIENTATION GUIDE:
==================
HORIZONTAL COLLAGES (Side-by-Side Layout):
- Dimensions: 1200x675 pixels (16:9 ratio)
- Layout: Before on LEFT, After on RIGHT
- Best for: Wide shots, landscapes, house exteriors, roof overviews

VERTICAL COLLAGES (Top-and-Bottom Layout):
- Dimensions: 800x1067 pixels (3:4 ratio)
- Layout: Before on TOP, After on BOTTOM
- Best for: Tall shots, close-ups, detail work, vertical surfaces
